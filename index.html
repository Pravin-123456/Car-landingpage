About

The Porsche 911 GT3 is a high-performance sports car that features a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine producing 502 hp, paired with a motorsport-inspired chassis and advanced aerodynamics. With a redline of 9,000 RPM, it delivers exhilarating performance both on the road and the track, embodying Porsche's dedication to precision engineering and driving pleasure. It boasts a top speed of 296 km/h and accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.2 seconds.
